Coding Quiz

A dynamically populating interactive quiz website. Upon starting the quiz, a timer begins counting down from 30 seconds. For each correct answer a +1 tally is recorded and for each incorrect answer 10 seconds is taken off the timer. Upon completing the quiz the final score is presented and an option to save your score and initials to the high scores page. Buttons to the high score page and to restart the quiz at the end.
